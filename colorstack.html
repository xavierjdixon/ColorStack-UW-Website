Welcome to ColorStack UW!

Empowering Professional Development Through Diversity
Seattle
Tech Community

The University of Washington chapter of ColorStack connects and empowers Black and Latinx students pursuing technology careers. As part of a national tech nonprofit, we aim to increase the number of Black and Latinx Computer Science graduates through community building, academic support, and career development. Our mission is to equip students with the resources they need to complete their degrees and secure rewarding technical jobs, fostering a future where Black and Latinx technologists lead in innovation.
